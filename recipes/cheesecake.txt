<b>Cheesecake</b><br/><br/>
<i>The perfect dessert for the dairy meals of Shavuos, this will enhance your holiday to the next level!</i><br/><br/>

Ingredients:<br/>

For the Crust:<br/>

7 tablespoons (3 1/2 ounces/100 grams) unsalted butter, room temperature<br/>
2 large egg yolks<br/>
1 tablespoon dry instant decaffeinated coffee<br/>
1/2 cup sugar<br/>
1 1/2 cups cake flour<br/><br/>


For the Filling:<br/>

3 large eggs<br/>
3 1/3 cups (750 grams) Israeli white cheese, or quark<br/>
1 cup sugar<br/>
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla sugar<br/>
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour<br/><br/>


For the Topping:<br/>

8 ounces sour cream<br/>
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla sugar<br/>
2 tablespoons sugar<br/><br/>


Instructions:<br/>
1) Gather the ingredients.<br/>

2) Preheat the oven to 375 F. Spray a 9 x 13-inch cake pan with nonstick cooking spray.<br/>

3) In a bowl, combine butter, egg yolks, coffee, sugar, and flour with your hands until the mixture is crumbly.<br/>

4) Press the mixture into the bottom of the cake pan. This forms the base for the cake.<br/>

5) In a separate bowl, combine eggs, white cheese, sugar, vanilla sugar, and flour.<br/>

6) Pour this mixture on top of the cake base.<br/>

7) Bake for 30 minutes or until the cheese does not wiggle. Remove cake and cool for 30 minutes.<br/>

8) Turn oven temperature down to 200 F.<br/>

9) In a small bowl, mix sour cream with vanilla sugar and sugar. Spread evenly over the cooled cake.<br/>

10) Bake again for 20 minutes.<br/>

11) Refrigerate until fully cooled and firm and then serve.<br/>
