<b>Matzah Balls</b><br/><br/>

<i>This gluten free addition to your chicken soup will make your Pesach meals much more exciting!</i><br/><br/>

Ingredients:<br/>
3 to 4 potatoes, peeled and cooked, well drained<br/>
4 tablespoons oil<br/>
2 to 3 eggs, raw<br/>
1/2 teaspoon salt<br/><br/>

Instructions: <br/>
1) Mash potatoes.<br/>
2) Heat oil in skillet.<br/>
3) Brown potatoes, mixing constantly. Cool.<br/>
4) Add eggs and salt. Refrigerate for several hours.<br/>
5) Shape into balls and drop into boiling soup. Cook over moderate heat for 30 minutes.<br/>
